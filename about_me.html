About
Me

I'm Nahla Niavarani, a UX-UI Designer based in Vancouver. With an engineering background in the Medical Devices Industry, and having worked as a photographer and digital artist for about ten years, I strive to integrate my skills and passion for making a difference, to bring a unique value to the teams I work with.
I entered into the realm of UX in 2020, when I was working on a project to design and develop a Workflow Management Platform by VBA. I realized the need for understanding the users' needs and got interested in UX. This interest eventually led me to pursue my education in this area and brought me the opportunity to develop human-centered solutions that seamlessly blend my technical mindset with artistic flair.
After cooperating as a UX-UI Designer in three comprehensive academic projects at Langara College and working on several design studies, now I'm competent to take on new challenges in the industry. Let's connect, collaborate, and craft meaningful journeys together.
